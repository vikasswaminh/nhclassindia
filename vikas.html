Low FODMAP Meal Plan - Kidney & Sinus Friendly

About This Meal Plan

This meal plan is designed for individuals looking for a low FODMAP, gluten-free, dairy-free, and kidney-friendly diet. It avoids spicy foods, onion, garlic, fermented foods, and high-sugar fruits while ensuring easy digestion and sinus relief.

Base for Every Meal

✔ Steamed White Rice + Moong Dal Sabji + Selected Vegetables

✔ 1 tsp Isabgol (Psyllium Husk) added to every vegetable sabji

Approved Vegetables

  • Bottle Gourd (Lauki)
  • Capsicum (Green Bell Pepper)
  • Peas (in moderation)
  • Carrot
  • Pumpkin
  • Tinda (Indian Baby Gourd)
  • Karela (Bitter Gourd)
  • Palak (Spinach)

Daily Meal Plan

Breakfast Options

  • Soft Rice & Moong Dal Sabji
  • Poha with Peas & Carrots
  • Rice Porridge with Coconut Milk
  • Pumpkin Mash with Rice
  • Tinda Rice Bowl

Lunch Options

  • Rice + Moong Dal + Spinach Sabji
  • Lauki Sabji with Rice
  • Karela Sabji + Rice
  • Carrot & Peas Sabji with Rice
  • Light Moong Dal Khichdi

Dinner Options

  • Rice + Moong Dal + Capsicum Sabji
  • Plain Rice + Lauki Sabji
  • Pumpkin & Peas Stir-fry + Rice
  • Tinda Sabji + Rice
  • Rice + Palak Sabji

Anytime Snacks

  • Lite Multigrain Mix ✅ (Anytime snack)
  • Roasted Makhana (Fox Nuts)
  • Fresh Coconut Slices
  • Herbal Tea (Ginger/Peppermint/Chamomile)
  • Soft Steamed Sweet Potato
  • Plain Puffed Rice (Murmura) with Peanuts

Night Exception

✔ Knorr Korean Kimchi Soup (Moderate quantity, before 9 PM)

✔ 1 tsp Isabgol added to the soup for digestion

✔ Do not consume before sleeping (Ideal time: 1-2 hours before bedtime)

Foods to Avoid

  • Onion, Garlic, and Spicy Foods
  • Fermented Foods (Idli, Dosa, Dhokla, Pickles)
  • Sugary Fruits (Banana, Mango, Grapes, Apple)
  • Junk Food (Spicy Bhujiya, Maggi, Bakery Items)
  • Heavy Lentils (Rajma, Chana Dal, Urad Dal, Masoor Dal)
