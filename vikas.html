Ankylosing Spondylitis (AS) – Personal Diet & Inflammation Tracker

🔥 Inflammatory Response Pattern

  • Severe internal heat sensation (bone-deep) without external fever
  • Very high ESR levels
  • Spiking blood pressure
  • Pulse consistently over 100
  • First sign of recovery: pulse normalization

❌ Triggers to Strictly Avoid

  • All types of rice (white, brown, etc.)
  • All gluten-containing grains (wheat, maida, suji)
  • Millets (ragi, bajra, jowar)
  • Curd and sour dairy
  • Packaged items (to avoid excess salt, sugar, preservatives)

✅ 30-Day Healing Diet Plan

🌅 Morning (Breakfast)

  • Homemade dosa (gluten-free batter)
  • Subjis: Karela, Cauliflower, Cabbage, Shimla Mirch, Lauki, Tinda, Petha

🥗 Afternoon (Lunch)

  • Homemade dosa or gluten-free soft chapati
  • Homemade paneer sabji (light oil, no spices)
  • Boiled moong dal (no onion, garlic)

🌃 Evening (Dinner)

  • Light vegetable soup (home vegetables only)
  • Idli (fermented rice-lentil, gut-safe)
  • Chicken soup (once or twice a week)

🧘‍♂️ Additional Observations

  • Pulse rate drop signals inflammation recovery
  • Better sleep follows reduced inflammation
  • Emotional stress, junk food, and irregularity cause flare-ups

📝 Purpose of This Page

To document and remember effective anti-inflammatory dietary patterns and triggers to manage Ankylosing Spondylitis without relying heavily on medications. This is a living page for tracking health trends and sustaining healing through food and routine.
